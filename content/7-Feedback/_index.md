### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The learning and working environment at AWS is highly dynamic and professional. Everyone is extremely sociable and willing to share knowledge, which helped me integrate quickly right from the early days. The biggest plus is the open culture with no significant gap between Admins and interns. The Admins always provide support, answer questions, and communicate very openly on WhatsApp.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The Admins are incredibly dedicated and closely follow the progress of our work. Instead of micromanaging, they often suggest documentation or keywords for me to research on my own, which greatly sharpens my independent problem-solving skills. The Admins are also very enthusiastic, quick to respond, and thoroughly support any inquiries regarding paperwork and procedures.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The Labs we learn are closely aligned with real-world environments, allowing me to directly apply my acquired knowledge to solve practical system problems.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
The internship not only helped me elevate my technical skills but also enabled many interns to significantly improve soft skills like public speaking. The weekly Community Events allow interns to exchange and share useful knowledge with one another. Many share everyday insights that are never redundant during the event to encourage participants.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The teamwork among members is fantastic. When a project enters a crucial phase or when bugs arise, everyone rolls up their sleeves and collaborates to achieve common goals. The members of the intern group also actively support one another throughout the learning process.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The compensation policies are very good compared to the market. The company provides flexibility in terms of studying and attending events so I can easily balance my university schedule.
