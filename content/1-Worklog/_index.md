**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Getting familiar with AWS and basic AWS services](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [VPC Networking — building the multi-AZ network foundation](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [EC2, Docker & Systems Manager Session Manager](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [RDS PostgreSQL (Multi-AZ)](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [S3 Static Hosting & CloudFront CDN](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Application Load Balancer & Launch Template](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Auto Scaling Group (dynamic scaling)](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [ElastiCache Redis (application caching)](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [SQS + Lambda + SES — order processing for Maison Édition](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [DynamoDB — activity log for Maison Édition](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [CloudWatch + SNS + WAF — monitoring & edge security for Maison Édition](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Cost optimization, cleanup & wrap-up for Maison Édition](1.12-week12/)
